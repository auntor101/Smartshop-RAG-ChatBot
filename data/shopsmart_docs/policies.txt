ShopSmart BD Policies

Return Policy
ShopSmart BD accepts returns for most eligible products within 30 days of delivery. To qualify for a return, the item must be unused, unwashed, undamaged, and returned with original tags, packaging, manuals, accessories, warranty cards, and invoices. Items that are damaged after delivery because of misuse, improper installation, or accidental handling are not eligible for return.

Customers may request a return from the Order History page by selecting the relevant order and item. ShopSmart BD provides free return pickup for eligible items where courier pickup is available. If pickup is not available in the customer's area, support will provide the nearest drop-off option.

Excluded Items
Some items cannot be returned unless they arrive defective, damaged, or incorrect. Excluded items include perishables, groceries, intimate apparel, personal hygiene items, opened cosmetics, customized products, downloadable digital goods, gift cards, and clearance items marked final sale. Products missing serial numbers, warranty stickers, original tags, or included accessories may also be rejected during inspection.

Exchange Policy
ShopSmart BD may offer replacement for defective, damaged, or incorrect items if stock is available. If replacement stock is unavailable, the customer may receive a refund according to the refund timeline.

Refund Timeline
After the returned item passes inspection, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. bKash refunds are usually processed within 2 hours after approval. Nagad, card, PayPal, and bank refunds may take longer depending on the payment provider. Shipping fees are refunded only when the return is caused by a ShopSmart BD error, damaged item, defective item, or incorrect shipment.

Privacy Policy Summary
ShopSmart BD collects customer information needed to process orders, deliver products, provide support, prevent fraud, and improve services. This may include name, phone number, email address, delivery address, payment confirmation details, device information, and order history. ShopSmart BD does not sell customer personal information. Customer data may be shared only with trusted delivery partners, payment processors, fraud prevention services, and legal authorities when required by law.

Customers may request correction of inaccurate account details through Account Settings or by contacting support. ShopSmart BD uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect customer information, but customers are responsible for keeping their passwords and one-time passwords private.

Dispute Resolution Process
If a customer has a problem with an order, product, delivery, return, refund, or payment, the customer should first contact ShopSmart BD support with the order ID, contact number, and a short explanation of the issue. Support will acknowledge the dispute and review order records, courier updates, payment logs, and submitted evidence such as photos or unboxing videos.

Most disputes are resolved within 3-5 business days. Complex cases involving courier investigation, supplier inspection, or payment provider review may take up to 10 business days. If the customer is not satisfied with the initial decision, the case may be escalated to a support supervisor for final review.

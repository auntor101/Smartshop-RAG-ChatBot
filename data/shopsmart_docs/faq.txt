ShopSmart BD FAQ

1. What delivery options does ShopSmart BD offer?
ShopSmart BD offers standard delivery in 3-7 business days and express delivery in 1-2 business days in eligible areas.

2. How much does shipping cost?
Shipping fees depend on your delivery address and selected delivery speed. Orders over BDT 1000 qualify for free standard shipping.

3. Do you offer free shipping?
Yes. ShopSmart BD provides free standard shipping on orders over BDT 1000.

4. Can I choose express delivery?
Yes. Express delivery is available for selected locations and usually arrives within 1-2 business days.

5. How long does standard delivery take?
Standard delivery usually takes 3-7 business days after order confirmation.

6. How can I track my order?
You can track your order using your order ID on the ShopSmart BD website. We also send SMS alerts when your order is confirmed, shipped, and out for delivery.

7. I did not receive my tracking SMS. What should I do?
Check that the phone number on your account is correct. If the number is correct and the SMS still has not arrived, contact support with your order ID.

8. What is the return window?
Most eligible items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

9. Are returns free?
Yes. ShopSmart BD offers free returns for eligible items within the 30-day return window.

10. What condition must returned items be in?
Returned items must be unused, unwashed, undamaged, and include the original tags, packaging, manuals, and accessories.

11. How do I start a return?
Sign in to your account, open Order History, select the item, and choose Request Return. You can also contact support with your order ID.

12. Can I return used products?
No. Items must be unused and in their original condition with tags attached.

13. Which payment methods are accepted?
ShopSmart BD accepts Visa, Mastercard, bKash, Nagad, PayPal, and cash on delivery in Dhaka only.

14. Is cash on delivery available everywhere?
No. Cash on delivery is available only for delivery addresses inside Dhaka.

15. Can I pay with bKash or Nagad?
Yes. You can pay securely with bKash or Nagad during checkout.

16. Can I change my payment method after placing an order?
Payment method changes are not guaranteed after checkout. If your order has not shipped, contact support as soon as possible.

17. How do I reset my password?
Click Forgot Password on the sign-in page and follow the reset link sent to your registered email address.

18. How do I change my email address?
Sign in to your account, go to Account Settings, and update your email address. You may need to verify the new email before it becomes active.

19. Where can I see my order history?
Your order history is available in your ShopSmart BD account under Order History.

20. How do I apply a promo code?
Enter your promo code in the Promo Code box at checkout and click Apply before placing the order.

21. Why is my promo code not working?
Promo codes may expire, have minimum order values, or apply only to selected products. Check the code terms and make sure it is entered exactly as provided.

22. Does ShopSmart BD have a referral program?
Yes. You receive BDT 200 referral credit for each successful referral after the referred customer completes an eligible purchase.

23. Are ShopSmart BD products authentic?
Yes. ShopSmart BD provides an authenticity guarantee for products sold directly by ShopSmart BD and verified marketplace partners.

24. What warranty do electronics have?
Eligible electronics include a 6-month warranty unless a product page states a different official brand warranty.

25. What warranty do accessories have?
Eligible accessories include a 1-month warranty unless the product page states otherwise.
